Welcome to Chore Tracker!

To Member Login/Registration

Chore tracker is your go to site for managing, assigning and tracking household chores. To begin, please register as the parent of your household. Once registered, you can create your very own household, and share your unique household code with your children to create their own accounts!

If you are a child of an existing household, please navigate to our Member Login and Registration in the top right corner. Make sure you have your household ID handy!
