To Parent Login/Registration

Chore tracker is your go to site for tracking your assigned chores! To begin, please register as a member of your household below. You will need the household ID from your parent. Once registered, you will be able to view and finish your assigned chores! Please remember your username and password as you will need these to login.

If you are a parent of an existing household or would like to register and create a new household, please navigate to our Parent Login and Registration in the top right corner.
